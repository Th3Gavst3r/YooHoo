What information is collected

YouTube access tokens and playlist IDs are stored indefinitely until the user issues a deregistration.

Discord user and channel identifiers are stored indefinitely until the user issues a deregistration.

How information is used

YouTube account and playlist information is used solely to update playlist contents according to the user's YooHoo registration.

Discord user identifiers are used to authorize interactions with YooHoo and to issue important notifications to users.

Discord channel identifiers are used to associate posted videos with a playlist registration.

How information is shared

YooHoo does not share any of your information with any outside actors.
